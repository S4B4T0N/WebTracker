Privacy Policy – WebTracker

WebTracker does not collect personal data.

The extension measures browsing activity such as:

  • visited URLs
  • time spent on pages
  • network usage

These data are:

  • stored anonymously
  • associated only with a random installation ID
  • not linked to any personal identity

Data may be sent to Supabase for analytics purposes.

No personal information (name, email, passwords) is collected.

The extension does not sell or share user data with third parties.
