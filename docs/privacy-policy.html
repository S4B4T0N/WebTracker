Privacy Policy for WebTracker

WebTracker ("we", "our", "the extension") respects user privacy and is committed to protecting it.

1. Information We Collect

WebTracker may collect the following information required for its core functionality:

  • visited website URLs,
  • website hostnames (domains),
  • time spent on each website,
  • network activity metrics related to the active tab.

2. How We Use the Information

The collected data is used solely for:

  • tracking browsing time,
  • displaying usage statistics to the user,
  • analyzing browsing activity within the extension.

3. Data Storage

Data may be stored locally in the browser and, where enabled, remotely in a secure backend environment for analytics purposes.

Each installation may be associated with an anonymous identifier. No direct personal identity information is required for the extension to function.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade or intentionally share user data with third parties for advertising purposes.

5. Personal Data

WebTracker is not designed to collect personal identity information such as:

  • name,
  • email address,
  • financial information,
  • passwords.

6. Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect stored data. However, no digital system can guarantee absolute security.

7. User Control

Users can remove the extension at any time, which stops future data collection through the extension.

8. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future to reflect product or compliance changes.

9. Contact

For support or privacy-related questions, replace this line with your preferred contact email or support URL before publishing.
